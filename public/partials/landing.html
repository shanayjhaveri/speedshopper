intro:

We created SpeedShopper to help you be more efficient when shopping at Walmart. On our website, you follow a very simple process to create a list of what items you need to buy and we then give you the best route to take in-store to pick up all the items you need.

Recommendation Feed

On our main feed page, you can look up items from the Walmart catalog and add them to your cart. We also have some sweet recommendations for what you are most likely to buy so feel free to check them out. You can also convert other users' lists and recipes into a set of items that you can add to your shopping cart.

Find your Path

Click on the create map button on the main feed page to generate a map of Walmart that shows you the most efficient path to take when you enter your local Walmart. As you pick up your items, your cart will update to show what items you have left to buy and where to go in order to get them so you can go back to your life.
